Ben J. Ryder

Chief Executive Officer
Geeza Electric Car

ABOUT

Ben Ryder is the CEO of the rising electric car provider in California,
the Geeza Electric Car, where he manages the overall operations.
Before establishing Geeza, Ben was the Operations Manager for YCL
Manufacturing Company in Georgia. With a background in
electronics, he later established Geeza Electric Car in 2050.

Ben earned an electrical engineering degree at Stamps Engineering
University. He spent 2 years with Brim Electronics Company, where
he trained and interned to build his portfolio before getting a real
job in 2053, Geeza Electrical Car was awarded the Substainable Future
Award for its vision towards greener Earth.

Apart from managing Geeza, Ben is active in sports, such as golf and
fencing. He has been a member of the California Fencing
Association since 2045.
